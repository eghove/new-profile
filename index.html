About Me.

Full-Stack Web Developer & Writer

My name is Eric Hove, and I'm a full-stack web developer and writer living in St. Paul, Minnesota.

I live with my wife, Julie, and our two shy cats: Marcel and Beckett (yes, they're named after two famously reclusive writers). I've worked for the U.S. Government for nearly 10 years and am currently exploring a career change. I'm a graduate of Hamline University with a B.A. in Economics and a M.F.A. in Creative Writing. I'm the author of the poetry chapbook Strange Friend, published by Red Bird Chapbooks (2015).

In my spare time, I enjoy reading, writing, walking, trips to the Boundary Waters, visits The Nook and The Groveland Tap, and re-watching Blade Runner to fret about humanity's ethically confusing future. I also serve as Board Secretary for Academic Arts High School, a small charter school in West St. Paul.
