About Me.

Full Stack Web Developer & Writer

My name is Eric Hove, and I'm a full stack web developer and writer in Saint Paul, Minnesota.

I live with my wife, Julie, and our two shy cats: Marcel and Beckett. I've worked for the U.S. Government for nearly 10 years and am exicted to work as a full stack web developer. I'm experienced in HTML5, CSS3, Javascript, JQuery, JQuery UI, Bootstrap, Firebase, Node Js, MySQL, MongoDB, Express, Handlebars Js, & React Js, and enjoy solving complex technical problems to create stunning web applications.

In my spare time, I enjoy reading, writing, walking, trips to the Boundary Waters, and re-watching Blade Runner to fret about humanity's ethically confusing future. I also serve as Board Secretary for Academic Arts High School, a small charter school in West St. Paul.

Thank you for visiting, and I hope to hear more from you soon!
